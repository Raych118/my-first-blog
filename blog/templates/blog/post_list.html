Welcome Ladies & Gentlemen

This blog focuses on THE SCIENCE OF RELIGION in relation to THE STRING THEORY which is a subdivision of THE UNIFIED FIELD THEORY .... Newton died trying to solve this piece of the puzzle he had been provided by his study of physics and the universe. Here we air our views on how these two topics relate and how one can inherently feed on the other. Some would even argue that the reason Newton failed to solve this mystery and died trying was because he failed to accept that not everything runs on science and all he had to do was turn his mind slightly to involve the spiritual to discover what exactly lay behind this force he realised governed the world as a "bubble universe" (in scientific terms).
